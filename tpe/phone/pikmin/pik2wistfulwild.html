the wistful wild is the 4th and final area of pikmin 2. there are no new pikmin types
before everything. you should know that there's a gattling groink. it will head for your base. you need to kill it AND bring it back to an onion or else DEATH MURDER DESTRUCTION
when you exit the starting area, you'll come to a fork. on the right is a wall, a hermet crawmad, and some water. going to the left, you'll be greeted by a decorated cannon beetle. jesus crisps
to the left of the cannon beetle, you will find the cavern of chaos. on top of the platform next to the cannon beetle, theres a few dwarf bulborbs, and a bridge on the other side of a lake. the lake has a few wollywogs...
on the other side of the lake is a firey blowhog, and a cloaking burrow-nit. in the stump ahead, theres two orange bulborbs, and a puffy blowhog. AUGH
pushing through the stump, you will find the dream den. glorious cave that dream den
on a path next to the stump, and behind the poison wall near the dream den, is an area with like 3 creeping chrysanthemums... and in the very back of the map is the hole of heroes... so you know the impact site from pikmin 1
uh... backtracking to spawn, you know the wall that was in the water? behind that is an area with 3 burdgeoning spiderwarts, a drain rock, and an eletric gate. behind the electric gate is a cannon beetle larve... and a shortcut to the hole of heroes! :D
