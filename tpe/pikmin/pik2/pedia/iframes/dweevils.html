firey dweevil



-- bosses --

dweevils are little guys who walk on four legs and steal things by picking them up! you have to throw pikmin onto the object to get it off of the dwevil after it's stolen... after that, throw a purple pikmin on the dweevil to stun it, or just throw a bunch of pikmin while standing directly under the dweevil.
purple pikmin will ALWAYS stun every non-boss dweevil.

extremely distant relatives of the firey bulblax. shoots fire when provoked
