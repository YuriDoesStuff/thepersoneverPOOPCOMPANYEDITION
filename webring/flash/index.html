a macromedia/adobe flash webring!

this webring is for people who like flash!! any site that utilizes flash is welcome here - from small animations, to full music videos, to stuff that even bends what flash is
if your site doesn't use flash, that's fine too - i might be able to help create a widget for you. if you come up with an idea and are able to draw assets, you can email or dm me to get in touch! it is preferred that you make your widget if possible, though

to create a widget, you can base your code off of one of the template FLAs: (flash 8/cs6). then, once you've finished making it, send your customized FLA to thepersonever.net on the nekoweb discord, or send a link to download it to jadeharley0409(at)proton(dot)me!
note: to use your widget you must have ruffle and swfobject on the webpage with your widget. if you need help setting up your widget, again, feel free to contact me!

rules

- your widget must contain an interactive element
- neither your site nor widget may contain malicious code
- your widget may not contain autoplaying audio (ruffle DIES (desyncs) if you autoplay audio)
