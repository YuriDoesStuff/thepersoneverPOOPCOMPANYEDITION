a macromedia/adobe flash webring!

this webring is for people who use flash in their websites! any site that utilizes flash is welcome here - from small animations, to full music videos, to stuff that even bends what flash is

to add your site to this webring, create a widget based off of this fla, then send your customized fla to thepersonever.net on the nekoweb discord, or send a link to download it to jadeharley0409(at)proton(dot)me
note: to use your widget you must have ruffle and swfobject on the webpage with your widget

rules

- your site must include flash content
- your widget must contain an interactive element
- if your site has adult content, you must state so on your landing page, and mark your widget with an 18+ disclaimer
- neither your site nor widget may contain malicious code
- your widget may not contain autoplaying audio
