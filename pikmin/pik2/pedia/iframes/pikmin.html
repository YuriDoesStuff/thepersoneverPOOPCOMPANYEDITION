red pikmin



these cute little much-less-then-pint sized plant creatures are your key to survival on this hostile planet! they can ward off enemies, create more of themselves, carry various things, and even die i guess.

each and every pikmin has their very own onion! purple and white onions aren't in this game though... mainly due to balancing, and possibly to continuing continuity. onions are a special place where defeated enemies can be carried back to, and turned into more pikmin! :D

red pikmin arent the greatest in this game... theyre able to destroy fire things without too much pain, and have 1.5x the attack damage that all other pikmin have (except for purples. they're insane). hazards are much less deadly in pikmin 2 than in the previous game though, due to having more control over your pikmin, the fact that fire takes 5 seconds to kill a pikmin now, and especially because you can destroy every elemetal hazard with any pikmin type. overall, i'd say they're still useful, but are the least useful type of pikmin in pikmin 2.
