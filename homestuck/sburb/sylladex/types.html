-- various types of moduses --

as stated in the overview, moduses define how your sylladex works.

to choose which modus you would like to view, click one of the buttons below!

the array modus is the most simple. you can store items in any of the cards, and extract all of them at any time. hard to weaponize due to that, though...

the stack modus allows you to put your most recently captchalogued at the top of the deck, while only allowing you to take out the top card.

if you captchalouge something when your deck is full, the oldest card in your deck will be ejected!

the queue modus lets you put your most recently captchalouged card at the top of your deck, but only lets you access your oldest captchalouged card

grabing a card while your inventory is full will eject your oldest item at tremendous speeds!

to explain this modus, i like to think of it as a filesystem... the tree modus allows you to store items under each other, as if they were folders

each captchalouged card is its own folder, and taking one card out will result in the cards within to also be ejected

there are two settings you can manipulate; auto-balance and which card you take from.

for which card you can take from, you can choose between taking out the root card, or taking one of the top leaves!

you can figure out how the auto-balance system works for yourself :D

the hashmap modus enters items with user-defined names into an also user-defined hasher, which dictates what card your captchalouged item goes into!

ejection occurs when you captchalouge an item with the same value,

or when you... yell out a word with the same value as an item in your sylladex. i guess

the settings you can define on a hashmap modus are what values the hash generates with each leter, and detecting colisions.

detecting colisions prevents you from picking up items that will eject other captchalouged items from your deck!
