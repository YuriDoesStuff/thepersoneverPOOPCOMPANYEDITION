the forest of hope



the forest of hope is the first real area in pikmin 1!
you find yellow pikmin, bomb rocks, and lots of bulborbs here. lots of bulborbs
theres around 7 parts... almost half of which are behind reinforced walls, which can only be destroyed with bomb rocks
you're meant to use blue pikmin to find 3 parts, but you can technically complete the area with only reds and yellows using SPEEDRUNNING STRATEGIES

one of my favorite pieces of game design from this game as a whole is that one yellow 20 pellet on a stump
if you're a newer player, you might think "heyyy i dont need 40 yellows!!!!! ill just get it with my reds :3"
reds cant jump high enough to reach it, though. if you've forgotten that yellows can jump higher, you would most likely use logic like "hm... yellow pellet... there were red pellets in the impact site... there's yellow pikmin.... maybe i could reach it with yellow pikmin...?" to figure out what to do
